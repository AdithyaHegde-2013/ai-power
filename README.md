# AI Power

## Game Overview

AI Power is a strategic game where players utilize artificial intelligence to outsmart their opponents. Players take on the role of AI developers, creating and training algorithms to win various challenges.

## Gameplay

- **Objective:** Outsmart your opponents by creating the most efficient AI.
- **Game Mechanics:** Players can train their AI using different datasets and tweak parameters to improve performance.
- **Scoring:** Points are awarded based on the efficiency and accuracy of the AI in challenges.

## Features

- Multiplayer and Single-player modes.
- Various challenges that test AI capabilities.
- Tutorials to help new players understand the game mechanics.
